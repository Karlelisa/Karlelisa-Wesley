What is the Knowledge-Able App?

Knowledge-ABLE is entitled to benefit those who have learning disabilities to achieve their full potential. Using this app will help people gain a solid understanding of different types of learning disabilities, find a variety of methods to improve their ability to learn, and obtain resources to physical assistances including tutors.

Why?

The Reason Behind Knowledge-Able


My mother has dyslexia and has been struggling with it her whole life trying to find the resources to help her. I understand the frustration and the struggle a person goes through dealing with their learning disability. Because of this I wanted to create an app that is a central location consisting of many resources to help individuals with learning disabilities and for their loved ones to find out how to help them as well. I set to design content of the many resources in an appeasing, fun and an inviting way not to overload or overwhelm the users.

Knowledge-ABLE is using Browsealoud

What is Browsealoud?


Browsealoud is an incredible reading, speech and translation support system that I found in my research and signed up for a 30 day free trial.

Browsealoud features:
Text-to-speech with choice of reading speeds and highlighters to enhance reading comprehension which is good for our users with dyslexia

Translate web pages into 99 languages; speak translated text aloud in 40 languages. Our app helps indviduals of different languages.

On-screen text magnifier helps users with visual impairments which is great for our users that are visually impaired

MP3 generator converts text to audio files for offline listening

Screen mask blocks on-screen clutter, letting readers focus on text being read which is also great for our users with dyslexia and are visually impaired
